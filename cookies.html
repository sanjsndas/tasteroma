What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and to remember your preferences. For example, cookies can remember your login details, language settings, and shopping cart items. They allow websites to recognize your device and store some information about your past actions or preferences. This makes your next visit easier and the site more useful to you. Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

Beyond that, cookies help us understand how you interact with our content, enabling us to improve our services and tailor your experience. Our use of cookies is always aimed at providing you with a better, more personalized, and more efficient browsing experience.
